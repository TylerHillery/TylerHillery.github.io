Quincy Larson, founder of [freeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/learn), created a new year's resolution challenge called [2022 Become-a-Dev](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/2022-become-a-dev-new-years-resolution-challenge/) which consists of of 4 steps.

1. Max out your stats in Learn ToCode RPG
2. Start #100DaysOfCode (which I already [started](https://github.com/TylerHillery/100-days-of-code/blob/master/log.md))
3. Complete the freeCodeCamp Relational Database Certification
4. Publish 3 tutorials on your Hashnode blog (I plan on publishing mine here.)

If you are up for the challenge quote-retweet this [tweet](https://twitter.com/ossia/status/1476581551873925123) with the words "I'm in."

Also checkout my #100DaysOfCode log. Recently I have been learning a lot about Python & I write about helpful insights that I pick up on the way. Good luck with the challenge!
