Summary

My original review

This book does a great job of providing real world examples of data pipeline work. I can already see myself referring back to it in the future when I am looking for examples of how certain things are done. The author does a great job outlining reusable patterns that can be applied broadly. Very quick read and a book I would definitely recommend to other data professionals or those that want a better idea of day to day data engineering work.

ChatGPT Revision

This book excels at demystifying the intricacies of data pipelines with a wealth of real-world examples. Its practicality shines as it becomes a future reference for understanding how various tasks are accomplished. A swift and insightful read, I highly recommend this book to both data professionals and anyone interested in gaining a clearer insight into the day-to-day world of data engineering.
